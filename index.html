About

We build companies from scratch

We don't invest in startups — we build them. Seedwork originates ideas, tests them against real markets, and scales what works. No pitch decks. No waiting for founders to show up. We are the founders.

Every venture begins with a thesis about where value is being extracted unnecessarily. We find the structural inefficiency, design a fairer model, and move fast to prove it.

Our ventures span food tech, language learning, consumer AI, and physical retail — connected not by industry, but by a shared conviction that better economics create better products.

Ventures

What we're building

BudMedia

Live

Zero-cost branded packaging, funded by local advertising

Restaurants receive professionally printed, branded pizza boxes at zero cost. Local businesses advertise on a medium that sits on kitchen tables for 30 minutes and reaches 2.8 people per box — unskippable, unblockable, and unmissable.

MadBud

In Development

Food delivery where the commission goes back to customers

MadBud collects the standard 15% commission and returns it entirely to consumers as an automatic discount. On a typical 250 DKK order, customers save over 100 DKK compared to major platforms. Restaurants use their own drivers — no courier fleet, no inflated prices.

Core1000

In Development

Learn 1,000 words. Understand 80% of everyday conversation.

Language learning rebuilt on corpus linguistics. 1,000 high-frequency words in strict frequency order, paired with 40 reusable sentence templates and powered by FSRS — the most accurate spaced repetition algorithm in existence.

Grain

Validating

Real-time AI content detection for everyone

A browser extension that scans social media feeds in real time, flagging deepfakes, AI-generated media, and false claims before they spread. Privacy-first: zero data collection, no tracking, no ads — ever.

SnackBud

Validating

Rethinking vending and snack distribution from the ground up

An early-stage exploration into physical retail — vending and snack distribution. The thesis: find where margin is being extracted unnecessarily and rebuild the model around the people actually involved.

Process

How we build

01

Identify

We scan markets for structural inefficiencies — places where middlemen extract value without earning it. Every venture starts with a thesis.

02

Validate

Before building anything, we test demand with real users and real money. If the signal isn't there, we kill it early and move on.

03

Build

Small teams, fast iteration, zero excess. We ship MVPs measured in weeks, not quarters. The product is the proof.

04

Scale

Ventures that survive validation get the resources to grow — operational infrastructure, partnerships, and the capital to go further.
