Philosophy

Most startups fail because they build before they validate. We don't. Seedwork runs multiple ideas in parallel, using real signals — revenue, signups, unit economics — to decide what lives and what dies.

Core1000

Learn 1,000 words. Understand 80% of everyday conversation.

Language learning built on corpus linguistics, not gamification. Teaches the highest-frequency words in strict order, paired with 40 reusable sentence templates and driven by FSRS spaced repetition. A coverage meter shows one number: the percentage of real conversation you now understand.

  • Templates first, words second — patterns generate thousands of natural exercises
  • Four-stage mastery: Introduction → Recognition → Recall → Production
  • Intelligent error detection with animated corrections for word order, negation, gender
  • Movie and series courses for contextual vocabulary beyond the core
  • Launching in Danish. Next: Spanish, English, Portuguese, French, Indonesian

BudGruppen

Three ventures. One thesis: cut the middleman.

A group of lean, product-focused businesses built out of Sønderborg, Denmark. Each targets a different inefficiency in how local economies move money, food, and attention.

BudMedia

Free branded pizza boxes for restaurants, funded by local advertising printed on the surface. Sits on a kitchen table for 30 minutes, reaches 2.8 people per box. Can't be blocked or skipped.

MadBud

Food delivery where the 15% commission goes back to the customer as an automatic discount. Restaurants use own drivers. 7 DKK platform fee only. Over 100 DKK saved per typical order vs. Wolt or Just Eat.

SnackBud

Early-stage exploration into vending and snack distribution. The physical retail layer of the BudGruppen ecosystem.

Grain

See what's real.

Consumer AI content detection tool — browser extension and mobile app that scans social media feeds in real time, flagging deepfakes, AI-generated media, and false claims before they spread.

  • 8M deepfakes online, human detection accuracy at just 24.5%
  • Passive feed scanning on YouTube and Instagram — no extra steps
  • Privacy-first: zero data collection, no ads, no tracking — ever
  • Freemium: free daily scans, Grain Pro at $4.99/mo for unlimited
  • Detection data trains proprietary models → API → enterprise licensing
  • Chrome extension MVP in development, traction via waitlist
