Philosophy

Don't build stuff nobody wants.

I'm a student. I don't have money to burn on ideas that might not work. So I don't. I test everything first, will people actually pay for this? If yes, I build it. If no, I move on. Simple.

I'm studying electrical engineering at SDU in Sønderborg, and Seedwork is basically my way of running real businesses while still in school. Every project here started as a question, not a product.

About Seedwork

Seedwork is my umbrella for all the business ideas I'm testing and building. I'm Barnabas, an electrical engineering student at SDU in Sønderborg, Denmark.

The idea is pretty simple, I run a few projects at the same time, figure out which ones people actually want, and focus on those. Everything else gets shelved.

Right now I'm working on stuff in food delivery, ad tech, language learning, and AI. Not because I planned it that way, but because those are the problems I kept running into.
